Top 20 Most Popular Anime Openings (August 2024)

Anime openings are more than just theme songs—they set the tone, evoke nostalgia, and, for many fans, become anthems of their lives. From the electric energy of Guren no Yumiya (Attack on Titan) to the emotional waves of Unravel (Tokyo Ghoul), anime openings transcend borders, uniting fans worldwide through music.

For me, anime openings are more than just background tracks; they’re my go-to playlist. I listen to them when I work, when I train, and when I need motivation. The way these songs fuse powerful instrumentals, deep emotions, and unforgettable melodies makes them an essential part of my everyday life. This project is my way of celebrating the most legendary anime openings—the ones that have dominated playlists, racked up millions of streams, and left a lasting impact on anime culture.

Using Proportional Symbol Visualization, I’ll showcase the top 20 most popular anime openings as of January 2025. The bigger the circle, the more popular the song—allowing us to visually grasp which openings have made the biggest mark in anime history.
